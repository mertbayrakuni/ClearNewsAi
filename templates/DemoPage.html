Tips for Identifying Fake News

  • Check the source: Verify the credibility of the news outlet.
  • Read beyond the headline: Ensure the story is not misleading.
  • Check the author: Look for author credentials and expertise.
  • Examine the date: Ensure the news is current and relevant.
  • Validate with multiple sources: Cross-check with trusted sources.
